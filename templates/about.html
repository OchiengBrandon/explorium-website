Our Story

Who We Are

Explorium Organisation is dedicated to promoting Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education in Kenya. We believe that STEM education is a catalyst for economic empowerment and innovation.

Founded with a vision to position STEM as an important catalyst in the economic empowerment of individuals and communities, we have been working tirelessly to enrich schools' curriculum and equip learners by providing valuable hands-on learning experiences.

Our world-class exhibits are designed to increase learners' skills and understanding of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, making education more engaging and practical.

Our Mission

To promote Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) in Kenya by providing innovative educational experiences that inspire curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking in learners of all ages.

We are committed to enriching the school curriculum and equipping learners with practical skills through hands-on experiences, while also providing a platform for families to explore science together.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in promoting STEM education in Kenya, fostering a generation of innovative thinkers and problem solvers who will drive the country's economic and technological advancement.

We envision a future where STEM education is accessible to all, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, and empowering individuals to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values
