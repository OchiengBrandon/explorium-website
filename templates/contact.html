Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Explorium Organisation.

Explorium Organisation is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education in Kenya. We provide hands-on learning experiences, teacher training, and family engagement programs to foster a love for STEM subjects.

Schools can partner with Explorium Organisation through our School Partnership Program. This program offers various opportunities including workshops, field trips, teacher training, and access to our STEM resources. Please contact us through the form above or email us at partnership@explorium.org for more information.
